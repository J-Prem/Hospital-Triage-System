# 🚀 AI-Powered Smart Hospital Triage & Scheduling System

An AI-driven healthcare solution designed to improve emergency response and hospital workflow efficiency by intelligently prioritizing patients and assisting hospital staff with real-time insights.

---

## 🏥 Hackathon Overview

This project was developed during the **32-Hour AI Hackathon conducted as part of Pragyan'26 at the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Trichy**, in collaboration with **Kanini Software Solutions India Pvt. Ltd.**

The hackathon focused on building **innovative AI solutions for real-world problems**, and our project aims to address critical challenges in **hospital triage, patient prioritization, and healthcare workflow optimization**.

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Hospitals often face:

- 🚨 Delayed emergency response due to inefficient triage
- ⏳ Long patient waiting times
- 👨‍⚕️ Uneven doctor workload distribution
- 📋 Manual patient prioritization
- 📄 Difficulty interpreting medical data quickly

These challenges can lead to **delayed treatment for critical patients**, affecting healthcare outcomes.

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution

The **AI-Powered Smart Hospital Triage & Scheduling System** uses artificial intelligence to:

- Analyze patient symptoms and medical data
- Classify patient risk levels instantly
- Prioritize emergency cases
- Recommend the appropriate medical department
- Assist hospital staff with real-time operational insights

The system ensures that **critical patients receive faster medical attention while optimizing hospital resources.**

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🤖 AI-Based Risk Prediction
Analyzes symptoms, vitals, and medical history to classify patients into:

- 🟢 Low Risk
- 🟠 Medium Risk
- 🔴 High Risk

---

### 🎤 Voice-Enabled AI Health Assistant
A conversational AI chatbot that allows patients to:

- Describe symptoms using voice or text
- Receive health guidance
- Generate quick health analysis reports
- Get answers to common health-related questions

---

### 📊 Smart Admin Dashboard

Provides hospital staff with:

- Real-time patient queue monitoring
- Risk-based prioritization
- Doctor workload tracking
- Smart alerts for emergency cases
- Operational visibility across departments

---

### 📷 AI Medical Image Analysis

Supports medical scan uploads such as **X-rays**, where AI models assist in detecting potential abnormalities and generating diagnostic insights.

---

## ⚙️ System Workflow

1️⃣ Patient logs into the system  
2️⃣ Starts a **health assessment**  
3️⃣ Inputs **symptoms, vitals, and medical history**  
4️⃣ AI model analyzes data in real time  
5️⃣ System predicts **risk level and department recommendation**  
6️⃣ Voice-enabled AI assistant provides guidance  
7️⃣ Admin dashboard monitors patient flow and priorities
